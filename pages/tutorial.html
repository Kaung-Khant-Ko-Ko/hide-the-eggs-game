Tutorial

Here you have the number of eggs left to hide on the right side. On the left side is the field to hide your eggs in.

After you put an egg on the field, the number of eggs left to hide decrease on the right side. You can remove the egg on the field if you want to change the place.

After you have hid all the eggs, you can click ready button to start playing.

Here we have two fields. The left field is your field with the eggs you hid. You have to find three eggs on the field on the right side. The game is turn-based. So after you search one area, it's the computer's turn.

The text below the field on the left side shows your current score.

The text below the field on the right side shows the score of the computer.

If you found three eggs first, you win.

If the computer found three eggs first, you lose.
